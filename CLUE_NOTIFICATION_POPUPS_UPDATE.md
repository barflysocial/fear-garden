# Clue Notification Popups Restored

This update restores clue pop-up notifications while keeping clue content hidden from the popup.

Behavior:
- When evidence unlocks, the player gets a popup notification.
- The popup says which investigation app/icon was updated.
- The popup does not show the clue title or clue text.
- The player must open the designated investigation icon/app to read the clue.
- Round reminders and round briefing popups still work.
