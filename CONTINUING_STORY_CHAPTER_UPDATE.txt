Fear Garden Continuing Story Chapter Update

This build changes Fear Garden from separate difficulty-only levels into one connected five-chapter story arc.

Story model:
- Chapter 1: The Planter Fall — Training Detective
- Chapter 2: The Garden Toast — Rookie Detective
- Chapter 3: The Cold Room Contract — Junior Detective
- Chapter 4: The Misting Line — Detective
- Chapter 5: The Root Ledger — Senior Detective

What changed:
- Truth pack titles now read as story chapters.
- Difficulty still increases normally by chapter.
- Each chapter now includes saga metadata, story arc summary, previous chapter connection, and next chapter hook.
- Opening narration now frames each case as part of the larger Root Ledger saga.
- Public clues now include the chapter and Root Ledger thread.
- Round dialogue/objectives now remind players to consider the larger story.
- Reveal scripts now close the current chapter and tease/resolve the larger arc.
- Answer key explanations now include a saga connection.

What did not change:
- The app/page structure remains the same.
- Host/player/check-in flow remains the same.
- Sponsor popups remain available.
- Returning Play Now logic remains available.
- No node_modules folder is included.
