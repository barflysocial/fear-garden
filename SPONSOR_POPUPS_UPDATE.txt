Fear Garden sponsor popup update

Added host-side Automated Sponsor Popups manager.

Host can add, enable/disable, and delete sponsor popups per session.
Each sponsor popup includes:
- Ad title
- Ad message
- Ad timing

Supported timings:
- Waiting Room / Before Briefing
- After Round 2 Checkpoint
- After Round 4 Checkpoint
- Before Final Accusation
- Case Closed Screen

Player behavior:
- Sponsor ads use the normal popup OK flow.
- Sponsor ads do not interrupt Detective Notes.
- Sponsor ads do not pop over active checkpoint answering.
- Queued sponsor ads release after notes/checkpoint interaction ends.
- Disabled sponsor ads do not show to players.
