# Terms & Conditions Update

Added a required Terms & Conditions acknowledgment before RSVP and before entering the game with an access code.

Acknowledgment includes:
- Barfly.Social / BARFLY.SOCIAL LLC name, likeness, trademark/copyright permission language
- Fictional gameplay notice
- Venue/person non-accusation language
- Cellphone, Wi-Fi, carrier data, battery, browser, and device usage acknowledgment
- Player responsibility and venue rules language

Server routes now require `termsAccepted: true` for RSVP and access-code join requests.
