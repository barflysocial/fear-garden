Fear Garden Case Briefing

05:00

Mid City Beer Garden was supposed to feel alive tonight — lights tangled through the trees, glasses sweating on tabletops, strangers laughing over the low hum of music and patio chatter.

Then the mood changed. A guest tied to the evening’s private mystery promotion was found dead near the garden side of the venue, and the first officers on scene noticed something strange: the evidence did not point to one clear suspect. It pointed everywhere.

The victim had argued with someone over money, crossed paths with staff, exchanged messages that no longer line up, and left behind a digital trail that appears edited after the estimated time of death. Receipts disappeared. Phone activity shifted. A final message was sent when the victim should not have been able to send it.

You are the detective assigned to sort the noise from the truth. When the countdown ends, the 30-minute investigation begins automatically. Open each investigation app as evidence unlocks. Compare timelines, statements, messages, maps, money, and motive. When the final accusation opens, choose carefully.
